Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

The Shepparton Malayalee Association Inc. ("SHEMA", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect through this website (shema.com.au) and our membership registry, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

What we collect

  • Membership registration: full name, email, mobile number, residential address, profession, spouse/dependent details.
  • Contact form / email: your name, email, and any message you send us.
  • Website analytics: we may use privacy-respecting analytics (e.g. Netlify Analytics) that do not track individual visitors or set cookies.

Why we collect it

  • To manage SHEMA membership and communicate about events, fees, and community activities.
  • To respond to enquiries.
  • To improve the website experience.

Who sees your data

Your personal information is accessible only to authorised SHEMA committee members. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except where required by Australian law.

How we store it

Membership data is stored in a secure, access-controlled database (Firebase/Firestore). Access is restricted to authenticated administrators. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

How long we keep it

We retain your information for as long as your membership is active, plus a reasonable period afterwards for record-keeping. Contact us to request earlier deletion.

Your rights

You can:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data at any time.

Contact us

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, email us at sheppartonmc@gmail.com.
